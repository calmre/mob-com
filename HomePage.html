Westhaven College of Arts & Sciences

"Inspiring Creativity, Cultivating Knowledge"

Vision for 2024

To be a leading institution that nurtures innovative thinkers and creators, fostering a vibrant community where art and science converge to drive positive change.

Mission for 2024

Westhaven College of Arts & Science is dedicated to providing an interdisciplinary education that empowers students to explore their passions, engage in critical inquiry, and contribute meaningfully to society. We commit to fostering a collaborative environment that promotes creativity, diversity, and lifelong learning.

Programs Offered

Arts

  • Fine Arts: Painting, Sculpture, Photography, Digital Media
  • Performing Arts: Theater, Dance, Music Performance, Music Composition
  • Creative Writing: Fiction, Poetry, Screenwriting, Non-Fiction

Sciences

  • Natural Sciences: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Environmental Science
  • Social Sciences: Psychology, Sociology, Anthropology, Political Science
  • Mathematics and Statistics: Calculus, Statistics, Data Analysis

Interdisciplinary Studies

  • Art and Technology: Digital Art, Game Design, Multimedia Production
  • Environmental Studies: Sustainability, Ecological Design, Conservation
  • Health Sciences: Public Health, Health Psychology, Nutrition

Professional Development

  • Communication: Public Speaking, Media Studies, Graphic Design
  • Business and Management: Arts Management, Entrepreneurship, Marketing
  • Education: Art Education, Science Education, Curriculum Development
